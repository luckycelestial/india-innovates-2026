Executive Abstract

PRAJA is an AI-powered civic intelligence layer designed to modernize the feedback loop between citizens and administrators. By deploying an omnichannel intake protocol (WhatsApp, Voice, and Web) integrated with Bhashini AI, it democratizes access to grievance reporting while equipping nodal officers with predictive sentiment triggers via SentinelPulse maps.
