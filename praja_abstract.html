Executive Summary

PRAJA is an AI-powered Citizen Grievance and Constituency Intelligence Platform designed to bridge the structural gap between the public and civic administrators. By deploying a multi-channel intelligence layer (WhatsApp, Web, and SMS), the platform democratizes access to reporting while equipping local governments with predictive sentiment analysis. The core engine utilizes advanced Natural Language Processing (LLM) to classify, prioritize, and route grievances in near real-time, ensuring accountability and data-driven civic response.

Core Protocol & Infrastructure

Omnichannel "GrievanceOS"

A centralized intake system allowing citizens to report issues natively via voice-notes or text. Integrated with Bhashini AI for seamless multi-lingual transcription across Indian dialects.

SentinelPulse Intelligence

A GIS-integrated administrative dashboard featuring real-time grievance density heatmaps. Empowers ward-level officers with early-warning sentiment triggers.
