Executive Abstract

PRAJA is an AI-powered intelligence layer designed to modernize civic accountability. By deploying an omnichannel protocol (WhatsApp, Voice, and Web), it bridges the fractured communication loop between citizens and administrators. Utilizing Bhashini AI for vernacular linguistic sovereignity and LLM-based neural nodes for sub-second classification, it transforms raw grievances into actionable administrative data.
